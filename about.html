About Me

I am a brittle asthmatic sufferer, have been since I was two years old. But since I was 18, I have taken pride in helping others in my situation so people don't go through this alone.

I was one of the youngest patients to be put on the treatment Xolair. The dose is worked out for each patient on their IgE levelsl I have four injections, every two weeks.

Although I am allergic, I pride myself with the care I give to my animals. Possibly not the most healthiest of choices, but they are my mental strength.
